Description:

A technical quiz is a knowledge-based competition designed to test participants' understanding of technical concepts, problem-solving skills, and awareness of recent advancements in various fields like engineering, computer science, electronics, and technology.

Round 1: Technical MCQ Round

Rules and Regulations:

  • Each team will be given a question paper containing 20 multiple-choice questions.
  • Only one correct option should be selected for each question.
  • Each correct answer carries 1 mark; there is no negative marking.
  • Use of mobile phones, laptops, internet, or calculators is prohibited.
  • Top-scoring teams will qualify for the next round.

Round 2: Buzzer Round

Rules and Regulations:

  • Each team will be asked questions one by one.
  • The team that presses the buzzer first gets the chance to answer.
  • Correct answers will fetch points; wrong answers may lead to deduction.
  • Teams must not press the buzzer before a question is completely asked.
  • Any form of misbehavior or arguing with the quiz master will lead to disqualification.
