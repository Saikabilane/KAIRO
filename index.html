PSG COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY

Established in 1951, PSG College of Technology is an autonomous, government-aided, private engineering college in Coimbatore, India. It is affiliated with Anna University and accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with an 'A+' grade.

The institution is known for its strong industry-institute collaboration, innovative teaching methods, and state-of-the-art facilities. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in various fields of engineering, technology, and applied sciences.

PSG Tech has consistently been ranked among the top engineering institutions in India and has produced numerous alumni who have excelled in various fields globally.

ECEA

The Electronics and Communication Engineers Association (ECEA) is a student-run organization at PSG College of Technology that focuses on promoting technical knowledge and innovation in the field of electronics and communication engineering.

ECEA organizes various technical events, workshops, and seminars throughout the year to enhance the skills of students and keep them updated with the latest advancements in the field. The association provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and collaborate on innovative projects.

Through its activities, ECEA aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, preparing students for successful careers in the electronics and communication industry.
